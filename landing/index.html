How it works

Three steps. Live in under 30 minutes.

1

Point your chargers

Change the CSMS URL on each charger to wss://csms.chargepulse.in/ocpp/{cp_id}. We support OCPP 1.6J today, 2.0.1 in pilot. Works with Delta, ABB, Exicom, Servotech.

2

We learn your fleet

ChargePulse extracts 24 features per charger every 15 minutes and trains a per-station LSTM autoencoder. After 14 days of baseline, the model knows what 'normal' looks like.

3

Get alerts that matter

SMS, WhatsApp, email, Slack, webhook. With predicted failure type and the specific OCPP signal that triggered it. Resolve faster, prevent driver complaints.
